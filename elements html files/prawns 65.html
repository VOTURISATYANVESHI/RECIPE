Prawns 65 Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500 grams prawns, cleaned and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons cornflour
  • 1 tablespoon rice flour
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying
  • Curry leaves for garnish
  • Lemon wedges for serving

Procedure:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine yogurt, cornflour, rice flour, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, ginger-garlic paste, and salt to make a thick batter.
  2. Add cleaned and deveined prawns to the batter and coat them evenly.
  3. Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat.
  4. Deep fry the coated prawns in batches until they turn golden brown and crispy. Drain on paper towels.
  5. In a separate pan, heat a little oil and add curry leaves. Saut√© briefly until they become crisp.
  6. Add the fried prawns to the pan with crispy curry leaves and toss them together.
  7. Remove from heat and serve hot with lemon wedges.
