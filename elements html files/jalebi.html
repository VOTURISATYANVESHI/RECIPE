Jalebi Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 2 tablespoons gram flour (besan)
  • 1/2 cup yogurt (curd)
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • A pinch of saffron strands (optional)
  • Oil or ghee for frying

Procedure:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine all-purpose flour, gram flour, yogurt, and baking soda. Mix well to form a smooth batter. Let it rest for about 30 minutes.
  2. In a separate saucepan, combine sugar and water to make a sugar syrup. Bring it to a boil and simmer for about 5-7 minutes until it reaches a one-string consistency. Add cardamom powder and saffron strands (if using). Set the sugar syrup aside to cool.
  3. Heat oil or ghee in a deep frying pan over medium heat. You can use a jalebi dispenser or a piping bag to make jalebi shapes. If using a plastic bottle as a dispenser, make a small hole in the cap.
  4. Fill the jalebi dispenser with the prepared batter.
  5. Squeeze the batter into the hot oil in a circular motion to make spiral shapes. Fry until the jalebis are golden and crisp, flipping them to ensure even cooking. This may take about 2-3 minutes per batch.
  6. Remove the fried jalebis from the oil and immediately dip them into the sugar syrup. Let them soak in the syrup for a few seconds to absorb the sweetness.
  7. Remove the jalebis from the syrup and place them on a plate to cool. Repeat the process for the remaining batter.
  8. Your delicious Jalebis are ready to be served. Enjoy them warm or at room temperature!
