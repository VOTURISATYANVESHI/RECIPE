Baklava Sweet Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 package (16 oz) of phyllo pastry sheets, thawed
  • 1 1/2 cups unsalted butter, melted
  • 3 cups mixed nuts (walnuts, almonds, pistachios), finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Procedure:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, combine the finely chopped mixed nuts and ground cinnamon. Set aside.
  3. Brush a 9x13 inch baking dish with melted butter.
  4. Place one sheet of phyllo pastry in the baking dish and brush it with melted butter. Repeat this process, layering and buttering each sheet, until you have about 8 sheets layered.
  5. Sprinkle a thin layer of the nut mixture evenly over the phyllo pastry.
  6. Add another layer of phyllo pastry, brush with melted butter, and repeat the process until you have used all of the nut mixture.
  7. Top the baklava with the remaining phyllo pastry sheets, again brushing each sheet with melted butter.
  8. Using a sharp knife, cut the baklava into diamond or square shapes.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for about 50-60 minutes or until the baklava is golden brown and crisp.
  10. While the baklava is baking, prepare the syrup. In a saucepan, combine sugar, water, honey, and vanilla extract. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for about 10 minutes.
  11. When the baklava is done baking, remove it from the oven and immediately pour the hot syrup evenly over the hot baklava.
  12. Allow the baklava to cool and absorb the syrup for several hours or overnight before serving. This allows it to become sweet and sticky.
  13. Once cooled, carefully remove the individual pieces along the pre-cut lines and serve. Enjoy your homemade Baklava!
