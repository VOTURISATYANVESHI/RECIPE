Bread Omelette Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 small tomato, finely chopped
  • 1-2 green chilies, chopped (adjust to taste)
  • 2 tablespoons chopped bell peppers (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh coriander leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • A pinch of black pepper powder
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil or butter

Procedure:

  1. In a bowl, crack the eggs and beat them well with a pinch of salt and black pepper powder.
  2. Add the chopped onions, tomatoes, green chilies, bell peppers (if using), and fresh coriander leaves to the beaten eggs. Mix everything together.
  3. Heat oil or butter in a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  4. Place one slice of bread in the pan and pour half of the egg mixture evenly over it. Allow it to cook for a minute.
  5. Place the second slice of bread on top of the egg mixture and press it gently to form a sandwich. Flip the sandwich and cook until the eggs are fully cooked and the bread is golden brown on both sides.
  6. Remove the bread omelette from the pan and cut it diagonally into halves or quarters.
  7. Your delicious Bread Omelette is ready to be served! Enjoy it hot with ketchup or chutney.
