Veg Frankie Recipe

Ingredients:

  • For the Roti (Wrap):
    • 1 cup whole wheat flour
    • Water, as needed
    • Salt to taste
  • For the Filling:
    • 2 cups mixed vegetables (carrots, bell peppers, cabbage, etc.), finely chopped
    • 1 large onion, finely chopped
    • 2-3 green chilies, finely chopped
    • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
    • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
    • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
    • 1 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
    • 1 teaspoon garam masala
    • Salt to taste
    • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Mint chutney and tamarind chutney for spreading

Procedure:

  1. Prepare the roti (wrap) dough by mixing whole wheat flour, water, and salt. Knead until you have a smooth dough. Cover and let it rest for 20-30 minutes.
  2. Divide the dough into small, equal-sized balls and roll each ball into thin rotis. Cook them on a hot griddle or tava until they are cooked through and have brown spots. Keep them aside.
  3. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  4. Add finely chopped onions and green chilies. Sauté until the onions turn translucent.
  5. Stir in ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a few minutes until the raw smell disappears.
  6. Add finely chopped mixed vegetables, turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Cook until the vegetables are tender but still have a crunch.
  7. To assemble the Frankie, take a cooked roti and spread mint chutney and tamarind chutney on it.
  8. Place a portion of the cooked vegetable filling in the center of the roti.
  9. Roll up the roti tightly to form a wrap, folding in the sides as you go.
  10. Wrap it in a piece of foil or parchment paper to hold it together and serve hot.
