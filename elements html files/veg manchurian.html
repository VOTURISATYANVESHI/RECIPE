Vegetable Manchurian Recipe

Ingredients:

  • For Vegetable Balls:
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (carrots, cabbage, bell peppers, etc.), finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons cornflour
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying
  • For Manchurian Sauce:
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon tomato ketchup
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon cornflour mixed with 2 tablespoons water (for thickening)
  • Chopped spring onions for garnish

Procedure:

  1. For Vegetable Balls:
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine mixed vegetables, all-purpose flour, cornflour, soy sauce, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, and salt to form a dough.
  3. Shape the mixture into small balls and deep fry them in hot oil until they are golden brown and crispy. Drain on paper towels and set aside.
  4. For Manchurian Sauce:
  5. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add chopped onions, bell peppers, and minced garlic. Saut√© until they become soft and translucent.
  6. Add soy sauce, tomato ketchup, red chili powder, sugar, and salt. Mix well.
  7. Add water and bring the mixture to a boil.
  8. Stir in the cornflour-water mixture to thicken the sauce. Cook until the sauce reaches your desired consistency.
  9. Add the fried vegetable balls to the sauce and toss them until they are well coated.
  10. Garnish with chopped spring onions.
  11. Serve hot with steamed rice or noodles.
