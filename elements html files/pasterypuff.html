Puff Pastry Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 sheet of frozen puff pastry (thawed)
  • 1 egg (beaten, for egg wash)
  • For the Filling:
  • 1/2 cup cooked chicken (shredded)
  • 1/2 cup cooked vegetables (peas, carrots, etc., diced)
  • 1/4 cup grated cheese (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Roll out the thawed puff pastry sheet on a floured surface to make it slightly larger.
  3. Cut the puff pastry sheet into equal squares or rectangles, depending on your preference.
  4. In a bowl, combine the shredded chicken, cooked vegetables, grated cheese (if using), and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Place a spoonful of the chicken and vegetable mixture in the center of each puff pastry square.
  6. Brush the edges of the pastry with beaten egg to help seal the pastry.
  7. Fold the pastry over the filling to create a triangle or rectangle shape, then press the edges to seal.
  8. Place the filled pastries on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  9. Brush the tops of the pastries with more beaten egg for a golden finish.
  10. Bake in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes or until the pastries are puffed and golden brown.
  11. Remove from the oven and let them cool slightly before serving.
  12. Your homemade Puff Pastry delights are ready to be enjoyed!
