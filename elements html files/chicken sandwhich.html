Chicken Sandwich Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 4 slices of bread
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced onions
  • 4 slices of cheese (your choice)
  • Mayonnaise
  • Mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Butter for toasting

Procedure:

  1. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat a grill or a pan over medium-high heat and cook the chicken breasts until they are cooked through (about 6-7 minutes per side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F or 74°C).
  3. While the chicken is cooking, lightly toast the bread slices.
  4. Spread mayonnaise and mustard on the toasted bread slices.
  5. Layer lettuce leaves, sliced tomatoes, and sliced onions on two of the bread slices.
  6. Place a slice of cheese on each of the chicken breasts while they are still hot to allow it to melt slightly.
  7. Place the cheesy chicken breasts on top of the prepared bread slices with vegetables.
  8. Top with the remaining slices of bread to form sandwiches.
  9. Cut the sandwiches in half diagonally, and they are ready to serve!
