Mirchi Bajji Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 6-8 large green chili peppers (bajji mirchi)
  • 1 cup besan (gram flour)
  • 1/4 cup rice flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • Salt to taste
  • Water, as needed
  • Oil for deep frying

Procedure:

  1. Wash and slit the green chili peppers lengthwise, removing the seeds if you prefer less heat.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine besan, rice flour, red chili powder, cumin seeds, baking soda, asafoetida, and salt.
  3. Add water gradually and whisk to make a smooth batter with a consistency similar to dosa batter.
  4. Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat.
  5. Dip each green chili pepper into the batter, ensuring it's well coated.
  6. Gently slide the coated chili peppers into the hot oil and deep fry until they turn golden brown and crispy. Fry in batches if needed.
  7. Remove the fried Mirchi Bajji using a slotted spoon and place them on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  8. Serve hot with chutney or sauce of your choice.
