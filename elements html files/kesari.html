Kesari Sweet Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup semolina (rava or sooji)
  • 1/2 cup ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 cups water
  • A pinch of saffron strands (optional)
  • 1/4 cup mixed nuts (cashews, almonds, pistachios), chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder

Procedure:

  1. Heat ghee in a heavy-bottomed pan over low-medium heat.
  2. Add semolina (rava) to the melted ghee and roast it on low heat until it turns golden brown and releases a fragrant aroma. Stir continuously to avoid burning. This may take about 7-10 minutes.
  3. While roasting, in a separate saucepan, combine sugar and water. Bring it to a boil and simmer for about 5-7 minutes to make a sugar syrup. Add saffron strands (if using) and cardamom powder to the syrup. Set it aside.
  4. Once the semolina is roasted, add the chopped mixed nuts to it and mix well.
  5. Slowly pour the sugar syrup into the roasted semolina while continuously stirring. Be cautious as it may splatter.
  6. Keep stirring the mixture until it thickens and leaves the sides of the pan. This may take about 5-7 minutes.
  7. Your Kesari Sweet is ready! Remove it from the heat and let it cool slightly.
  8. Transfer the Kesari to a serving dish and level it. You can garnish it with additional nuts if desired.
  9. Enjoy your delicious Kesari Sweet warm or at room temperature!
