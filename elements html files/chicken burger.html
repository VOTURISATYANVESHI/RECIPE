Chicken Burger Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless chicken breast fillets
  • 4 burger buns
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced onions
  • Sliced pickles
  • Cheese slices (optional)
  • For Marinade:
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • For Sauce:
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon ketchup
  • 1 teaspoon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon honey

Procedure:

  1. In a bowl, combine olive oil, minced garlic, paprika, dried oregano, dried thyme, salt, and pepper to create the marinade.
  2. Marinate the chicken breast fillets in the mixture for at least 30 minutes (or longer for better flavor).
  3. Preheat a grill or grill pan over medium-high heat.
  4. Grill the marinated chicken fillets for about 5-7 minutes on each side or until fully cooked and grill marks appear.
  5. While the chicken is cooking, prepare the sauce by mixing mayonnaise, ketchup, mustard, and honey in a small bowl. Set aside.
  6. Toast the burger buns on the grill until they are lightly browned.
  7. Assemble the burgers by placing lettuce leaves, sliced tomatoes, onions, and pickles on the bottom half of each bun.
  8. Place a grilled chicken fillet on top of the veggies.
  9. If desired, add a cheese slice on top of the chicken while it's still hot.
  10. Spread the prepared sauce on the top half of the buns, then place them on top of the assembled burgers.
  11. Serve hot and enjoy your chicken burgers!
