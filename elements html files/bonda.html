Bonda Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups boiled and mashed potatoes
  • 1/2 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1/4 cup rice flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • A few curry leaves, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons chopped coriander leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying

Procedure:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the boiled and mashed potatoes, gram flour (besan), rice flour, cumin seeds, mustard seeds, turmeric powder, red chili powder, asafoetida (hing), chopped curry leaves, chopped coriander leaves, and salt to taste. Mix well to form a smooth dough. The dough should be firm enough to shape into balls.
  2. Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat.
  3. Take a small portion of the potato mixture and shape it into a round ball. Repeat this process to make more balls.
  4. Gently slide the potato balls into the hot oil and fry them until they turn golden brown and crispy. Fry them in batches to avoid overcrowding the pan.
  5. Remove the fried bondas from the oil using a slotted spoon and place them on a paper towel-lined plate to drain excess oil.
  6. Your delicious Potato Bondas are ready to be served! Enjoy them with chutney or sauce.
