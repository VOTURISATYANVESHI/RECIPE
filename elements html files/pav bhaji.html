Pav Bhaji Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 large potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, bell peppers, etc.), boiled and mashed
  • 1/2 cup chopped onions
  • 1/2 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons pav bhaji masala
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Chopped coriander leaves for garnish
  • 12 pav (small dinner rolls)

Procedure:

  1. Heat butter in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onions and saut√© until they turn translucent.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft and mushy.
  4. Stir in pav bhaji masala, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Cook for a few minutes until the spices are well incorporated.
  5. Add boiled and mashed potatoes and mixed vegetables. Mix well to combine.
  6. Mash the mixture using a potato masher or a wooden spoon while cooking. Continue to cook until the bhaji (mixture) is well blended and heated through.
  7. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
  8. For the pav, slit each pav horizontally and lightly toast them with butter on a griddle until they are golden brown.
  9. Serve the hot pav with the bhaji and a slice of lemon.
