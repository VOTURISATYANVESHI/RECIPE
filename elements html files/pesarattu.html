Pesarettu Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup whole green gram (moong dal), soaked overnight
  • 1/2 cup rice
  • 2-3 green chilies, chopped
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 inch piece of ginger, grated
  • A handful of fresh coriander leaves, chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for cooking

Procedure:

  1. Drain and rinse the soaked green gram and rice.
  2. Blend the green gram, rice, green chilies, and grated ginger together to make a smooth batter. Add a little water if needed.
  3. Transfer the batter to a mixing bowl and add chopped onions, chopped coriander leaves, cumin seeds, and salt. Mix well.
  4. Heat a non-stick griddle or frying pan over medium heat and lightly grease it with oil.
  5. Pour a ladleful of the batter onto the hot griddle and spread it into a thin circle to make a pancake.
  6. Cook until the bottom side turns golden brown, then flip it over and cook the other side until it's cooked through and crispy.
  7. Remove the pesarettu from the griddle and repeat the process with the remaining batter.
  8. Serve hot with coconut chutney or tomato chutney.
