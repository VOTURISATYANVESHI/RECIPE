Chicken Lollipop Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 12 chicken lollipops
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons cornflour
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • Oil for deep frying
  • Lemon wedges for garnish

Procedure:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine ginger-garlic paste, yogurt, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, and salt to make the marinade.
  2. Make deep cuts in the chicken lollipops to help the marinade penetrate.
  3. Coat the chicken lollipops with the marinade, ensuring they are well coated. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours (or longer for better flavor).
  4. In a separate bowl, mix cornflour and all-purpose flour.
  5. Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium-high heat.
  6. Dip each marinated chicken lollipop into the flour mixture to coat evenly, then shake off any excess flour.
  7. Gently place the coated chicken lollipops into the hot oil and deep fry until they are golden brown and crispy. Fry in batches if necessary.
  8. Remove the fried chicken lollipops from the oil and place them on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  9. Garnish with lemon wedges.
  10. Serve hot as an appetizer or snack.
