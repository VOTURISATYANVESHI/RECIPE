Idli Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup parboiled rice
  • 1/2 cup urad dal (black gram)
  • 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds (methi)
  • 1/4 cup poha (flattened rice)
  • Salt to taste

Procedure:

  1. Wash and rinse the parboiled rice, urad dal, fenugreek seeds, and poha separately in water. Soak them in water separately for about 4-6 hours or overnight.
  2. Drain the water from the soaked ingredients and grind them separately into a smooth paste using a little water. The urad dal paste should be fluffy, and the rice paste should be smooth.
  3. Combine both the urad dal and rice pastes in a mixing bowl. Add salt and mix well. The batter should be thick but with a pouring consistency. Cover the bowl and let it ferment for about 8-10 hours or overnight in a warm place.
  4. Grease the idli molds with a little oil or ghee. Pour the fermented batter into the molds, filling them about three-fourths full.
  5. Steam the idlis in a steamer for about 10-15 minutes until they are cooked and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  6. Remove the idlis from the molds using a spoon. They should come out easily if properly greased.
  7. Your soft and fluffy Idlis are ready to be served! Enjoy them with your favorite chutney and sambar.
