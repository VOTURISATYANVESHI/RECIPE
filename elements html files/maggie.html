Maggi Noodles Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 packet of Maggi noodles
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1/2 cup of mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, bell peppers, etc.), chopped
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 green chili, chopped (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish (optional)

Procedure:

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Allow them to splutter.
  2. Add finely chopped onions and sauté until they turn translucent.
  3. Stir in minced garlic and chopped green chili. Sauté for another minute until the garlic is fragrant.
  4. Add the mixed vegetables and cook for a few minutes until they start to soften.
  5. Season the vegetables with turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Mix well.
  6. Pour in 2 cups of water and bring it to a boil.
  7. Break the Maggi noodles into smaller pieces and add them to the boiling water.
  8. Let the noodles cook for about 2-3 minutes or until they are soft, stirring occasionally.
  9. Once the noodles are cooked, remove the pan from heat.
  10. Your delicious Maggi Noodles are ready to be served! Garnish with fresh coriander leaves if desired.
