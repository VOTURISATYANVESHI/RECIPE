Chicken Curry Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500 grams chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 large onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/4 cup cooking oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 4-6 cloves
  • 4-6 green cardamom pods
  • 2-inch cinnamon stick
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure:

  1. Heat oil in a large, heavy-bottomed pot over medium heat.
  2. Add cumin seeds, bay leaves, cloves, green cardamom, and cinnamon. Sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add finely chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a few minutes until the raw smell disappears.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft and mushy.
  6. Add chicken pieces and cook until they turn white.
  7. Stir in turmeric powder, red chili powder, and garam masala. Cook for a few minutes until the spices are well incorporated.
  8. Whisk yogurt and add it to the pot. Cook until the oil separates from the mixture.
  9. Add salt to taste and mix well.
  10. Simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the chicken is fully cooked and the curry thickens.
  11. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  12. Serve hot with rice or naan.
