Soan Papdi Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1 cup ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • A pinch of baking soda
  • 2 tablespoons chopped almonds (optional)

Procedure:

  1. In a heavy-bottomed pan, add ghee and melt it over low heat.
  2. Add gram flour (besan) to the melted ghee and roast it on low heat. Keep stirring continuously to avoid lumps and ensure even roasting. This may take about 15-20 minutes until it turns golden brown and gives off a nutty aroma. Remove from heat and set it aside.
  3. In a separate saucepan, combine sugar and water to make a sugar syrup. Bring it to a boil and simmer for about 5-7 minutes to form a one-string consistency syrup.
  4. Slowly pour the sugar syrup into the roasted gram flour while stirring continuously. Add cardamom powder and a pinch of baking soda. Mix well to combine. The mixture will thicken quickly, so work efficiently.
  5. Transfer the mixture onto a greased surface like a plate or a tray. Flatten it using a rolling pin while it's still warm to achieve a uniform thickness. Optionally, sprinkle chopped almonds on top and gently press them in.
  6. Let the mixture cool for a few minutes, then cut it into square or diamond-shaped pieces while it's still warm. Allow it to cool completely and set.
  7. Your delicious Soan Papdi is ready to be enjoyed! Store it in an airtight container for later consumption.
