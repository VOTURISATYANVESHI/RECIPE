Fish Fry Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 pieces of fish fillets (any white fish, like tilapia or cod)
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying
  • Lemon wedges for garnish

Procedure:

  1. Rinse the fish fillets and pat them dry with paper towels.
  2. In a shallow bowl, mix together all-purpose flour, red chili powder, turmeric powder, ground cumin, ground coriander, garlic powder, onion powder, and salt.
  3. Coat each fish fillet with the flour mixture, shaking off any excess.
  4. Heat oil in a frying pan over medium-high heat. The oil should be hot but not smoking.
  5. Gently place the coated fish fillets in the hot oil and fry for about 3-4 minutes on each side, or until they are golden brown and crispy.
  6. Remove the fried fish fillets from the oil and place them on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  7. Garnish with lemon wedges.
  8. Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce or side dishes.
