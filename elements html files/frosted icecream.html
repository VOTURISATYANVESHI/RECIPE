Frosted Ice Cream Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 cups your favorite mix-ins (chocolate chips, nuts, fruit, etc.)

Procedure:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine heavy cream, whole milk, granulated sugar, and vanilla extract. Mix until the sugar is completely dissolved.
  2. Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer's instructions. This typically takes about 20-25 minutes.
  3. During the last 5 minutes of churning, add your favorite mix-ins (e.g., chocolate chips, nuts, or fruit) and let the machine incorporate them into the ice cream.
  4. Once the ice cream reaches a soft-serve consistency, transfer it to an airtight container and smooth the top with a spatula.
  5. Cover the container and freeze the ice cream for at least 4 hours or until it's firm.
  6. Scoop and serve your delicious homemade frosted ice cream in bowls or cones. Enjoy!
