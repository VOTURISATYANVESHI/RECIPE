French Fries Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 large potatoes, peeled and cut into thin strips
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Salt to taste

Procedure:

  1. Wash the cut potato strips in cold water to remove excess starch.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a deep fryer or a heavy-bottomed pan to 350°F (175°C).
  3. Pat dry the potato strips using a kitchen towel to remove any moisture.
  4. Carefully add a batch of potato strips to the hot oil. Fry them until they are golden brown and crispy, about 3-4 minutes.
  5. Using a slotted spoon, remove the fried potato strips from the oil and place them on paper towels to drain excess oil.
  6. Repeat the frying process with the remaining batches of potato strips.
  7. While the fries are still hot, sprinkle them with salt according to your taste.
  8. Serve the French fries hot and enjoy!
