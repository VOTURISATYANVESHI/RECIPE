Chicken Dum Biryani Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500 grams chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup fresh mint leaves
  • 1/2 cup fresh coriander leaves
  • 1/4 cup cooking oil
  • 1/4 cup ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 4-6 green chilies, slit
  • Whole spices: 2 bay leaves, 4-6 cloves, 4-6 green cardamom pods, 2-inch cinnamon stick
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 cups water

Procedure:

  1. Wash and soak basmati rice in water for 30 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat ghee and oil in a large, heavy-bottomed pot over medium heat.
  3. Add cumin seeds and whole spices (bay leaves, cloves, green cardamom, cinnamon). Sauté until fragrant.
  4. Add thinly sliced onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.
  5. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies. Sauté for a few minutes until the raw smell disappears.
  6. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft and mushy.
  7. Add chicken pieces and cook until they turn white.
  8. Stir in red chili powder, turmeric powder, and garam masala. Cook for a few minutes until the spices are well incorporated.
  9. Whisk yogurt and add it to the pot. Cook until the oil separates from the mixture.
  10. Add chopped mint and coriander leaves. Mix well.
  11. Layer the soaked and drained rice on top of the chicken mixture in the pot.
  12. Add 2 cups of water and salt to taste. Bring it to a boil.
  13. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid, and let it simmer for 20-25 minutes. Do not lift the lid during this time.
  14. Turn off the heat and let the biryani sit for 10 minutes before opening the lid.
  15. Gently fluff the biryani with a fork, mixing the chicken and rice together.
  16. Serve hot with raita or salad.
